Recipe for preparing and enjoying Banku!

Banku is a popular Ghanaian dish loved mainly by the Akan and the Ewe tribes. It's normally eaten as lunch or supper. It can be taken with Okro stew or soup, hot pepper, or even light soup. I really this dish because it's very delicious, especially with Okro stew. Another reason why I like Banku is that it keeps you going the whole day.

Recipe to prepare Banku: My favourite food!

Banku is Ghanaian local dish popularly loved by the Ewe and Akan tribes.

In this write-up, I'm going to demonstrate how to cook Banku and the recipe needed to do that.

1. Ingredients to preparing Banku

  • Dough
    • Cassava dough and
    • Corn dough
  • Salt
  • Green pepper
  • Fresh tomatoes
  • Smoked fish or fresh fish

2. Steps to preparing Banku

  1. Mix the corn dough and the cassava dough in a 2:1 proprtion. The mixture should be fairly diluted.
  2. Add a reasonable amount of salt.
  3. Stir the mixture untill it becomes a paste and drive the paste untill cooked.
  4. Add water to the paste if it becomes too hard during the driving.
  5. Grind fresh pepper and tomatoes with salt. 🥵
  6. Add the smoked fish to the ground pepper and tomatoes. 👀
  7. Serve the pepper and the Banku. 👌
  8. Enjoy 😋! Thank me later! 🙏
